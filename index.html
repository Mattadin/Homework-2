About Me

Hello! My name is Matthew Fernandez, I'm originally from Miami, Florida in the US. I've been living in Australia since 2016 and have absolutely loved every bit of it.


I've worked in many different roles from hospitality, sales, electrician and pathology. My studies are focused predominantly in sciences and have recently set my sights on Web Development. Currently I'm studying at the coding bootcamp at University of Sydney to develop the skills required to meet client demand and expectations while creating spaces for companies or individuals to show off their own unique abilities or services!
